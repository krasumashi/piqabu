## 3. The Ghost ID model — no accounts, no recovery

When you first open Piqabu, the app generates a "Ghost ID" — a randomized identifier stored on your device. We use this identifier to route messages and apply your subscription entitlement. We do not link it to any real-world identity.

Because there are no accounts:

- **There is no password to recover.** If you uninstall the app, or factory-reset the device, your Ghost ID is destroyed and your subscription cannot be reactivated on a new device without re-purchasing.
- **There is no portability.** Your Ghost ID belongs to one device + one install. We do not provide a way to "transfer" it.
- **Clearing app storage may reset your identity** (depending on Android version and Piqabu version). We try to make this stable on supported Android versions, but cannot guarantee it across all device + OS combinations.

You should not rely on Piqabu as your only record of any conversation.
